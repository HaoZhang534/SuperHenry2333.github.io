3.Research on recommender system in ThinkLab in SJTU supervised by Junchi Yan(2018/10-2019/05)

4.Software security and reverse engineering research in Tencent (2018/7 2018/9)

5.Robust learning research supervised by Ping Yi, Shanghai Jiao Tong University(2018/3 2018/5)

We designed a method to detect adversarial examples for classification problem.
